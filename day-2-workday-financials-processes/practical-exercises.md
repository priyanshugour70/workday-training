### Day 2 – Practical Exercises

#### Exercise 1 – Map P2P/O2C to Workday Objects

- Draw a simple diagram:
  - For **P2P**: show requisition, PO, receipt, supplier invoice, payment.
  - For **O2C**: show order, invoice, cash.
- Next to each step, note:
  - Which **Workday object** it might be.
  - Which **spend category / GL account** could be impacted.

#### Exercise 2 – Spend Category & GL Brainstorm

- List 10 common expenses (e.g. office supplies, travel, software subscriptions).
- For each, propose:
  - A **spend category name**.
  - A high‑level **GL account** it would map to.

#### Exercise 3 – Approval Flow (Conceptual or Tenant)

- If you have access:
  - Open an example **supplier invoice** or **journal** and review the **business process history**.
  - Identify who approved and what would happen if a correction was needed.
- Without access:
  - Watch demo videos of Workday financial approvals.
  - Write a sample flow: Initiator → Manager → Finance → Complete.
