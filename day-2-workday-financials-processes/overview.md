### Day 2 – Workday Financial Processes & Configuration Basics

**Goal**: Understand key Workday Financials processes and basic configuration elements relevant to the JD.

#### Focus Areas

- High‑level **Financial processes in Workday** (P2P, O2C, Record to Report).
- **Spend Categories** and **General Ledger Accounts** (Chart of Accounts concepts).
- Basics of **Accounting Center** (conceptual).
- **Business process approvals, corrections, and re‑routing** for financial processes.

#### Outcomes by end of Day 2

- You can describe how **financial transactions** flow through Workday.
- You know what **spend categories and GL accounts** are and why they matter.
- You understand the idea of **approvals and corrections** for financial BPs.
