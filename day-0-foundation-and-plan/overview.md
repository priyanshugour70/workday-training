### Day 0 – Foundation & Learning Plan

**Goal**: Understand the Workday Financial Systems Specialist role, this JD, and design your personal 3–7 day study strategy.

#### What you will do today

- Read and break down the **Workday Specialist JD** (from the root `README.md`).
- Clarify the **scope of Workday Financials** and related tools (Prism, Accounting Center, Adaptive Planning, Reporting, SQL, Power BI).
- Review and refresh **finance and accounting fundamentals** you will need.
- Set **personal targets** for how many days (3, 5, or 7) you will use and how many hours per day.

#### Outcomes by end of Day 0

- You can explain, in your own words:
  - What a **Workday Financial Systems Specialist** does.
  - How the role **supports Controllership and Financial Systems**.
  - Why **data quality, reporting, and integrations** matter in this role.
- You have a **written micro-plan**: which days you will combine if you are doing 3–5 days instead of 7.
