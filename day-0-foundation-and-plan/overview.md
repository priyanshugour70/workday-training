### Day 0 – Foundation & Learning Plan (Very Detailed)

**Big goal for Day 0**: If you know nothing today, by the end of this day you should:

- Understand **exactly what this Workday Specialist role is** and how it works in real companies.
- See the full **Workday + data + finance big picture** end‑to‑end.
- Have a clear **study roadmap** for the rest of this repo (Days 1–6).
- Start thinking like someone with **7–8 years of practical mindset**, even if you don’t yet have that much hands‑on time.

Think of Day 0 as your **orientation + deep mental models** day.

---

Inside this folder you now have:

  - `glossary.md` – Workday + finance + data terms in one place.

---

#### How to Use Day 0 Step‑by‑Step

1. **Pass 1 – Big Picture Read**
   - Read `overview.md` (this file) fully once.
   - Read `theory.md` from top to bottom without worrying about memorizing.
2. **Pass 2 – Active Understanding**
   - Re‑read `theory.md` more slowly.
   - Stop after each major section and:
     - Draw the diagram in your own notebook.
     - Summarize in 3–4 bullet points in your own words.
3. **Pass 3 – Scenarios & Q&A**
   - Go to `qa-and-scenarios.md` and `practical-exercises.md`.
   - Read each scenario:
     - First try to answer yourself.
     - Then compare with the provided answer and reasoning.
4. **Pass 4 – Self‑Check**
   - Do the questions in `self-assessment-quiz.md`.
   - Mark where you are weak and revisit relevant sections of `theory.md`.
5. **Pass 5 – Plan the Remaining Days**
   - Use `checklist-and-outcomes.md` to confirm Day 0 concepts are solid.
   - Open the root `README.md` and quickly scan Days 1–6.
   - Decide how many calendar days (3/5/7) you will use and which days to combine.

If you do all 5 passes seriously, your **mental model** will be very close to someone with years of exposure (the remaining difference will mostly be real project experience, which later days will start to simulate).

---

#### Outcomes by End of Day 0 (Upgraded)

By the time you finish all Day 0 materials, you should be able to:

- **Explain the role clearly**:
  - Describe what a Workday Financial Systems Specialist does, who they work with, and why the role exists.
- **Draw the architecture from memory**:
  - Workday Financials Core → Accounting Center → Prism/Data Warehouse → Reporting/Power BI → Users.
- **Connect finance to systems**:
  - Take a simple finance scenario (e.g. paying a supplier) and talk through how it hits:
    - Business processes.
    - Accounting rules.
    - GL / reports / BI.
- **Talk like a near‑senior in interviews (conceptually)**:
  - Use the scenario answers in Day 0 to respond to:
    - Data mismatch questions.
    - Control / audit questions.
    - “Tell me about a time you improved a process” type questions (you’ll simulate them here).
- **Have a concrete study plan**:
  - Know exactly how you will attack Days 1–6 and in how many calendar days.

Once these are true, move on to Day 1. If not, stay on Day 0 longer and loop through scenarios and the quiz again.
