### Day 0 – Self-Assessment Quiz

Use this after reading `theory.md`, `practical-exercises.md`, and `qa-and-scenarios.md`.  
Try to answer without looking, then check your own answers against those files.

---

#### Section 1 – Concepts (Short Answers)

1. In your own words, what does a **Workday Financial Systems Specialist** do?
2. Draw (on paper) and then describe a simple diagram showing:
   - Workday Financials Core, Accounting Center, Prism, Data Warehouse, Reporting/BI.
3. What is the difference between a **spend category** and a **GL account**?
4. Why might a company use **both Prism and a data warehouse**?
5. Explain the term **data integrity** in this role. Give at least two practical examples.

---

#### Section 2 – Scenario Questions

6. A Controller tells you: “Supplier spend by category doesn’t match between Workday and Power BI.”  
   - List at least **three** possible causes.
   - List the first **three checks** you would perform.

7. Procurement wants to introduce a new **“Cloud Services”** spend category.  
   - What questions would you ask them before configuration?
   - Which parts of the system or process might need to change (at a high level)?

8. A new bank integration is going live. Name:
   - Two **business teams** you would work closely with.
   - Two **integration/data concerns** you would raise early.

---

#### Section 3 – Finance & Accounting

9. State the **accounting equation** and briefly define Assets, Liabilities, and Equity.
10. Describe how a **supplier payment** moves from:
    - Operational event → accounting entries → GL → financial reporting.

---

#### Section 4 – Tools & Data

11. Give an example of a simple **SQL query** (in pseudocode) that would:
    - Select company, account, and total amount from a journal table.
    - Only for one month and one company.
12. Why is **Power BI** useful to a Controller if they already have Workday reports?

---

#### How to Use Your Answers

- Compare your answers with:
  - Explanations and diagrams in `theory.md`.
  - Scenarios and model answers in `practical-exercises.md` and `qa-and-scenarios.md`.
- For any question where your answer feels weak:
  - Re‑read the relevant sections.
  - Try answering again **the next day** without looking.

Once you can answer all of these clearly and confidently, you are **Day‑0 ready** to dive into Day 1 at a much higher level than a typical beginner.
