### Day 0 – Glossary (Workday + Finance + Data)

Use this whenever you forget a term. Keep adding your own notes as you learn.

---

#### Workday / Finance Terms

- **Workday Financials Core**  
  Main Workday modules for finance: General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Assets, etc.

- **Accounting Center**  
  Workday component that converts **operational events** into **accounting entries** using configured rules.

- **Prism Analytics**  
  Workday analytics platform for blending **Workday data and external data** inside the Workday ecosystem.

- **Adaptive Planning**  
  Planning/budgeting/forecasting tool that integrates with Workday, used heavily by FP&A and Finance.

- **Business Process (BP)**  
  Configurable workflow in Workday defining steps, approvals, routing, and conditions for a process.

- **Spend Category**  
  Front‑end classification for spend (e.g. Travel, Office Supplies) that maps to one or more GL accounts.

- **Ledger Account (GL Account)**  
  Code representing a specific type of financial activity (e.g. Revenue, Cash, Rent Expense).

- **Company**  
  Workday object often aligned with a legal entity or reporting entity.

- **Cost Center**  
  Dimension representing departments or responsibility centers; key for managerial reporting.

- **Delegation**  
  Temporary assignment of approvals/tasks from one user to another.

---

#### Finance & Accounting Terms

- **General Ledger (GL)**  
  Central record of all financial transactions, organized by accounts and other dimensions.

- **Trial Balance**  
  Listing of all GL accounts and their balances at a point in time.

- **Financial Statements**  
  Formal reports (Balance Sheet, Income Statement, Cash Flow) derived from GL data.

- **Debits and Credits**  
  Two sides of every accounting entry; rules depend on account type (assets vs liabilities, etc.).

---

#### Data & Analytics Terms

- **Data Warehouse**  
  Central repository of structured data from multiple systems, optimized for reporting and analysis.

- **ETL / ELT**  
  Extract‑Transform‑Load / Extract‑Load‑Transform – processes used to move and transform data.

- **Fact Table**  
  Table containing transactional or numeric data (e.g. journal lines, invoices).

- **Dimension Table**  
  Table containing descriptive attributes (e.g. accounts, companies, cost centers).

- **Data Lineage**  
  The path data takes from its original source through all transformations to its final destination/report.

---

#### Tools Terms

- **SQL (Structured Query Language)**  
  Language used to query and manipulate relational databases (SELECT, WHERE, GROUP BY, JOIN, etc.).

- **Power BI**  
  Microsoft BI tool for creating interactive dashboards and reports.

- **EIB (Enterprise Interface Builder)**  
  Workday tool for building file‑based inbound and outbound integrations with less custom code.
