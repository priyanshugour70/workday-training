### Day 4 – Adaptive Planning, SQL & Power BI for Finance

**Goal**: Connect planning and analytics to Workday Financials and build hands‑on comfort with SQL and Power BI, as called out in the JD.

#### Focus Areas

- **Adaptive Planning** basics: models, versions, sheets, and how it ties to Workday.
- **SQL fundamentals** for querying finance‑style data.
- **Power BI** basics: connecting to data, building visuals and dashboards.
- How Controllership uses these tools for **forecasting, analysis, and decision‑making**.

#### Outcomes by end of Day 4

- You can describe **how Adaptive Planning complements Workday Financials**.
- You can write and understand basic **SQL SELECT queries** on tabular data.
- You can outline how to **build a simple Power BI report** using financial data.
