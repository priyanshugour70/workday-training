### Day 4 – Practical Exercises

#### Exercise 1 – Adaptive Planning Concept Mapping

- From public documentation or videos:
  - Identify how **actuals from Workday** are loaded into Adaptive.
  - Note how planners interact with **sheets** and **versions**.
  - Write a short paragraph: “In this JD, Adaptive matters because …”.

#### Exercise 2 – SQL Practice on Sample Data

- Use any public finance‑style dataset (e.g. CSV with transactions).
- Practice writing queries to:
  - Select key columns (date, account, amount, cost center).
  - Filter by date range and cost center.
  - Aggregate total amounts by account or cost center.
- Save example queries in your own notes.

#### Exercise 3 – Simple Power BI Report

- Load a sample dataset into Power BI (or equivalent BI tool).
- Build:
  - At least one **table visual**.
  - One **chart** (e.g. bar chart by cost center).
  - A **filter/slicer** for date or cost center.
- Think: what questions could Controllership answer with this report?
