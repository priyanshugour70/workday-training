### Day 6 – Practical Exercises

#### Exercise 1 – Mini Project Scenario

- Pick a scenario that touches multiple areas, for example:
  - “Controller wants a report of supplier spend by category and cost center, sourced from Workday and shown in Power BI.”
- On paper or in notes, design:
  - The **business requirement** in user‑story format.
  - High‑level **Workday config/reporting** changes needed.
  - **Data flow** to data warehouse / Power BI.
  - **Test cases** to validate the solution.

#### Exercise 2 – Troubleshooting Story

- Take any issue you practiced earlier (e.g. data mismatch between Workday and BI).
- Write a structured story:
  - Problem description.
  - Steps you took to investigate.
  - Root cause.
  - Fix applied.
  - What you learned / how you will prevent it in future.

#### Exercise 3 – Interview Prep (JD‑Mapped)

- For each major JD area (Workday Financials, Reporting/Prism/Accounting Center, Adaptive/Analytics, Integrations/EIBs, Data Integrity, SQL/Power BI, Soft Skills):
  - Write at least **one example** or explanation you can use in an interview.
  - Use **STAR format** where possible (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
