### Day 6 – Theory: Projects, Troubleshooting & Agile

#### 1. Small Projects Using Agile

- **Agile basics** relevant to this JD:
  - Work is broken into **user stories** (e.g. “As a Controller, I want a report that…”).
  - Work is planned in **sprints** (short time‑boxed periods).
  - Daily or regular **standups** to share status and blockers.
- For a Workday Specialist:
  - Clarify **requirements** with Finance/Controllership.
  - Collaborate with **IT and vendors** on technical tasks.
  - Provide **demos** and incorporate feedback.

#### 2. Troubleshooting Mindset

- When an issue is reported:
  - **Reproduce** the problem (or gather clear steps).
  - **Isolate** the area: configuration, data, integration, or user error.
  - Check **logs, reports, and data** (SQL/BI where appropriate).
  - Propose **workarounds** and **long‑term fixes**.
  - Document **root cause** and final resolution.

#### 3. Communicating with Non‑Technical Stakeholders

- Translate:
  - “System/technical language” → **clear business language**.
  - Emphasize **impact** (on financials, controls, deadlines).
- Use:
  - Simple diagrams.
  - Before/after examples.
  - Clear documentation and emails.
