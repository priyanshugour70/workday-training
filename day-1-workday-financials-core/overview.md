### Day 1 – Workday Financials Core & Navigation

**Goal**: Become comfortable navigating Workday Financials and understanding core objects relevant to this JD.

#### Focus Areas

- Workday **tenant navigation** (home page, search, related actions, reports).
- **Supervisory Organizations** and **Locations**.
- **Job Change templates**, **positions**, and **delegations**.
- Basics of **business process approvals** (viewing history, understanding routing).

#### Outcomes by end of Day 1

- You can **log in, navigate, and search** in Workday quickly.
- You understand the role of **supervisory orgs, locations, positions** in HR/Financial structures.
- You can explain at a high level how **job changes** and **delegations** work in Workday.
