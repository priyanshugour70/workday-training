### Day 1 – Checklist & Outcomes

#### Checklist

- [ ] Practiced basic Workday navigation (or watched equivalent demos).
- [ ] Reviewed **supervisory orgs** and **locations**.
- [ ] Understood the purpose of **job changes** and **delegations**.

- Be comfortable with **Workday terminology**: supervisory org, location, position, job change, delegation.
