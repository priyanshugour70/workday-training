### Day 1 – Practical Exercises

> Use a demo/sandbox tenant only. Never store real production screenshots or confidential data here.

#### Exercise 1 – Navigation Drills

- Practice:
  - Searching for workers, orgs, and locations.
  - Opening related actions for a worker or org.
  - Pinning frequently used reports or tasks to your favorites.

#### Exercise 2 – Supervisory Orgs & Locations

- Identify:
  - A supervisory org and its manager.
  - Workers in that org and their locations.
- Write down how this structure might impact **approvals** and **financial dimensions**.

#### Exercise 3 – Job Changes & Delegations (Conceptual if No Tenant)

- If you have access:
  - Walk through a **Job Change** business process (view only if possible).
  - View the **business process history** and approvals.
  - Review how **delegations** are configured for a user.
- If you do **not** have access:
  - Watch public demo videos on Workday job changes and delegations.
  - Draw a simple flow of: Initiator → Approver → Review → Complete.
