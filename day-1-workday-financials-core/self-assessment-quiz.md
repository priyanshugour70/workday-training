### Day 1 – Self-Assessment Quiz (Workers, Orgs, Job Changes, Delegations)

Try to answer without notes. Only look back at `theory.md`, `practical-exercises.md`, or `qa-and-scenarios.md` after you’ve tried.

---

#### Section 1 – Core Concepts

1. Explain the difference between:
   - Worker
   - Position
   - Job Profile
   - Supervisory Org
   - Location

2. Why is a **Job Change** more than “updating someone’s title”? Give at least two reasons.

3. What is a **Delegation** and why can it be both useful and risky?

---

#### Section 2 – Scenarios

4. A worker recently moved from Org A to Org B, but their expense approvals still go to the Org A manager.  
   - List at least three possible causes.  
   - Describe the first three checks you would make.

5. A Director wants to delegate all approvals to their assistant **for the entire year** to “save time”.  
   - What concerns do you have?  
   - How would you propose a better approach?

6. The company splits one supervisory org into two. What are:
   - Two main **configuration steps** in Workday?  
   - Two **finance or reporting** checks you’d want to run afterwards?

---

#### Section 3 – Navigation & BP

7. How would you find out **who currently has a Job Change task** that is “stuck”?

8. Without naming exact menu labels, describe the generic **path** you would take in Workday to:
   - Start from a worker.
   - Find their supervisory org and manager.
   - See the other workers in that org.

---

#### Section 4 – Reflection

9. If you could only check **three things** after a large org restructure to be confident nothing is badly broken, what would they be and why?

Use your own judgment to grade:

- If you cannot easily talk for **30–60 seconds per question** with structure, spend more time on Day 1 before moving on.
