workday-training
================

### Workday Financials Specialist – A to Z Training Plan (3–7 Days)

This repository is a complete, fast‑track training plan to become a **Workday Financial Systems Specialist** aligned to the following job description (JD). The focus is **Workday Financials, Prism, Reporting, Accounting Center, Adaptive Planning, data, integrations, and finance fundamentals**.

---

### Role Summary (Based on JD)

The **Workday Specialist** is a key member of the **Financial Systems / Controllership** team. You:

- **Support and enhance Workday Financials** (Core Financials, Prism, Accounting Center, Adaptive Planning, Reporting).
- **Partner with Finance, Accounting, and IT** to design and implement process and system improvements.
- **Work with data** (including a data warehouse) to ensure **data quality, integrity, and reporting**.
- **Configure, test, and implement** Workday configuration and integration changes.
- **Lead small projects using Agile** methods and work cross‑functionally with business and vendors.

---

### Essential Functions – Skills This Training Targets

- **Workday Financials & Related Tools**
  - Understand **Workday Financials Core**, **Prism**, **Accounting Center**, **Adaptive Planning**, and **Reporting**.
  - Learn key Workday objects: **Supervisory Orgs, Locations, Spend Categories, Ledger Accounts, Job Changes, Positions, Delegations**.
  - Practice **business process approvals, corrections, and re‑routing** in Workday.
  - Understand basics of **Workday integrations** (including **EIBs** and 3rd‑party/vendor/bank integrations).
  - Learn **Workday reporting basics**: filters, prompts, data sources, and simple calculated fields.

- **Finance & Accounting Knowledge**
  - Core **accounting principles**: debits/credits, GL, trial balance, financial statements.
  - How **Workday Financials** represents **accounts, spend categories, cost centers, companies, ledgers**.
  - Understand **financial processes** that Workday supports (P2P, O2C, Record to Report at high level).

- **Data, Reporting, and Analytics**
  - **Data warehouse** concepts: fact tables, dimensions, ETL basics.
  - **SQL fundamentals** for querying finance data.
  - **Power BI** basics: connecting to a data source, building visuals, dashboards, and simple measures.
  - How to **validate data**, **find data issues**, and **communicate findings** to Controllership teams.

- **Configuration, Integrations, and Testing**
  - How to **configure/test/implement** changes in Workday Financials (e.g., new spend category, GL account).
  - Understand the lifecycle of an **integration change** (requirements → design → build → test → deploy).
  - Learn to **document business requirements** clearly for IT and Finance Systems.
  - Practice **UAT scenarios**, test case writing, and defect tracking.

- **Ways of Working & Soft Skills**
  - **Agile basics**: epics, user stories, sprints, standups, retros.
  - **Stakeholder communication** with Finance, IT, vendors, and business users.
  - **Customer service mindset** and **issue troubleshooting** (root‑cause analysis).
  - **Documentation best practices** so business users can easily understand and follow procedures.

---

### Required / Preferred Qualifications – What You Will Build

This training plan is designed to help you demonstrate:

- **Finance/Accounting + Systems blend**
  - Solid grasp of **finance and accounting** concepts and how they appear in Workday.
  - Ability to translate **financial requirements** into **Workday configuration, reports, and data queries**.

- **Technical Skills**
  - Working knowledge of **SQL** and **Power BI** for financial data analysis.
  - Hands‑on familiarity with **Workday Financials** navigation and core business processes.
  - Understanding of **Workday EIBs**, **basic integrations**, and **reporting/data mapping** (Nice to Have in JD).

- **Professional Skills**
  - Strong **communication (verbal/written)** and ability to explain technical concepts to non‑technical users.
  - Ability to **prioritize**, **multi‑task**, and **meet deadlines**.
  - Ability to **support end users**, **troubleshoot issues**, and **work cross‑functionally**.

### Repository Structure – Day‑by‑Day Learning Path

This repo is organized into **Day 0 to Day 6** (7‑day track). You can compress this into **3–5 days** by combining multiple days if you have more time per day.

- **`day-0-foundation-and-plan/`**
  - Big picture of the **Workday Specialist role**, JD breakdown, and personalized learning strategy.
  - Review of **finance/accounting fundamentals** and core tools (SQL, Power BI, Workday basics).

- **`day-1-workday-financials-core/`**
  - Deep dive into **Workday Financials Core objects** and navigation.
  - **Supervisory Orgs, Locations, Job Changes, Positions, Delegations**, approvals.

- **`day-2-workday-financials-processes/`**
  - Workday **business processes** for Financials (approvals, corrections, re‑routes).
  - **Spend Categories, GL Accounts, Accounting Center** overview.

- **`day-3-reporting-prism-accounting-center/`**
  - **Workday Reporting**, **Prism**, and **Accounting Center** concepts.
  - Building simple reports and understanding **data mapping**.

- **`day-4-adaptive-planning-and-analytics/`**
  - **Adaptive Planning** overview and basic model concepts.
  - **Power BI** and **SQL** practice with finance‑style datasets.

- **`day-5-integrations-eibs-and-data-flows/`**
  - **Workday Integrations basics**, especially **EIBs** and 3rd‑party/vendor/bank data flows.
  - Understanding **data warehouse** role and **data quality checks**.

- **`day-6-projects-troubleshooting-and-interview-prep/`**
  - Mini **project scenarios**: requirement → design → config/report → test → documentation.
  - **Troubleshooting practice**, **Agile/project** exercises, and **interview preparation** mapped to this JD.

Each day folder contains multiple files such as:

- `overview.md` – what you must achieve that day.
- `theory.md` – concepts and explanations.
- `practical-exercises.md` – tasks to practice skills.
- `resources.md` – links (Workday docs, blogs, videos, generic resources).
- `checklist-and-outcomes.md` – what you should be able to do by end of day.

---

### How to Use This Repo (3–5 Day Version)

- **If you have 3 days**: Combine **Day 0 + 1**, **Day 2 + 3**, **Day 4 + 5 + 6**.
- **If you have 4–5 days**: Keep **Day 0** short, then combine **1+2** and **3+4**, with **5** and **6** as separate lighter days.
- **Always focus on outcomes**: by the end, you should be able to:
  - Walk through **Workday Financials** screens and core objects with confidence.
  - Explain how **financial data flows** from **Workday → Data Warehouse → Power BI**.
  - Write **simple SQL** queries and design **basic Power BI reports** for finance.
  - Describe how **EIBs and integrations** fit into the architecture.
  - Talk through **troubleshooting steps, documentation, and Agile work style** using JD‑aligned language.

You can expand each file with your own notes, screenshots (do not commit confidential data), and practice scenarios as you learn.
