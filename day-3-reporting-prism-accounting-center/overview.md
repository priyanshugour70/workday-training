### Day 3 – Workday Reporting, Prism & Accounting Center

**Goal**: Understand Workday reporting basics and the concepts of Prism and Accounting Center, focusing on how data becomes usable information for Controllership.

#### Focus Areas

- Workday **reporting fundamentals**: standard reports, prompts, filters, basic custom reports (conceptual).
- **Prism Analytics**: why organizations bring Workday + non‑Workday data together.
- **Accounting Center**: bridge from operational data to accounting entries.
- How reporting and these tools support **data integrity** and **financial analysis**.

#### Outcomes by end of Day 3

- You can describe **how a Workday report is built** at a high level (data source → fields → filters → prompts).
- You understand **what Prism and Accounting Center do** and when they are used.
- You can speak about **data lineage**: from operational activities to accounting and reports.
