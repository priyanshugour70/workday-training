### Day 3 – Practical Exercises

#### Exercise 1 – Analyze an Existing Report (or Demo)

- In a tenant or via demo videos:
  - Identify a **Workday report** relevant to finance (e.g. Trial Balance, Journal Lines, Supplier Invoices).
  - Note:
    - The **data source**.
    - Key **fields/columns**.
    - Any **filters/prompts** used.
  - Write a short explanation: “This report helps Controllership by …”.

#### Exercise 2 – Design a Simple Custom Report (On Paper)

- Pick a scenario, for example: “Show all supplier invoices over a certain amount with cost center and spend category.”
- On paper or in notes, design:
  - Data source you would use.
  - Fields and filters.
  - Prompts you would add for business users.

#### Exercise 3 – Data Lineage Story

- Choose a real‑world financial question, e.g. “What was total travel expense by department last quarter?”
- Write a **data lineage story**:
  - Source events (expense reports / supplier invoices).
  - How they become **accounting entries** (Accounting Center + rules).
  - How they end up in **reports** or **Prism** models.
