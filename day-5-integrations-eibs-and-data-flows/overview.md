### Day 5 – Integrations, EIBs & Data Flows

**Goal**: Understand how data moves between Workday, 3rd‑party vendors, banks, and the data warehouse, with special focus on EIBs and data integrity.

#### Focus Areas

- **Workday integrations** overview (types, patterns).
- **EIBs (Enterprise Interface Builder)** – basic concepts and use cases.
- Integrations with **3rd‑party vendors and banks** (payments, statements, HR feeds, etc.).
- Role of the **data warehouse** and how it works with Workday/Prism.
- Identifying, escalating, and helping remediate **data integrity issues**.

#### Outcomes by end of Day 5

- You can describe the main **integration patterns** involving Workday Financials.
- You understand what **EIBs** are and when they are typically used.
- You can tell a clear story of **Workday → Data Warehouse → BI/Power BI** data flow and where data quality checks fit.
